About us

Founded in December 2018, Ecotek’s mission is to cooperate with Ecopark’s vision of creating a leading model of a Smart City for Vietnam. We aim to harness the power of technological ingenuity such as blockchain, data analytics, and centralized computing in order to establish a fertile technical foundation, from which Ecopark can prosper its dream of establishing the nation’s first Smart City.

What we do

Application

EcoOne

EcoOne is a utility application with combined features that serve the needs of both residents and visitors in and out of Ecopark. With a focus on improving Ecopark’s efficiency and mobility and implementing our vision of building a Smart City, EcoOne acts as a digital information portal of all utilities, products and services available in and around Ecopark.

EcoBus

Ecopark currently routes 46 buses and 180.000 rides monthly. All busses are equipped with GPS and operates through smart bus cards that can be connected and managed on the EcoOne platform.

Merchants

Residents and visitors can find their next favorite go-to places by browsing through our merchant board that features all merchants’ information including products andservices offered, contact details, and working hours.

Navigation

Self-driving Cars

A partnership with Yamaha Company, FPT Corporation, and Ecotek introducted the Self-driving Car System - a smart transport solution for modern urban areas, bringing new experiences to Ecopark residents and visitors. Self-driving cars can be ordered our through smartphone app, routing from Westbay roundabout to the sports arena with a total of 3,4km/round at a top speed 15km/hour. Other features include: Slow down or stop when there is an obstacle in front of the vehicle. The display shows the vehicle performance and location indicators on the map.

Navigation

Electric Bikes

The Bike Sharing Sharing System is an intelligent application developed by Q Technology Joint Stock Company in cooperation with QIQ Vietnam Joint Stock Company. Just by opening the app and entering your destination, QIQ will find you the fastest route and the nearest charging station. The project will start with 60 vehicles and 4 charging stations located in both Phase 1 and 2.

Security

Security System

Ecotek Company coordinated, consulted with related departments to develop a security plan to ensure safety for Ecopark's luxurious residence - The Grand Islands. The security system for the villa complex is built into multiple layers including: Access control, Virtual security fence, Local Camera System and Internal security.

Card Service

All In One Card

All-In-One card (resident card) allows Ecopark residents access to utilities and services through the use of one single medium. The cards will be integrated with systems such as elevator dispatching, bus ticketing and clubhouses’ ticketing to manage access and use of services.
