We are currently looking for a Business Analyst to join our project team. You will work alongside other business analysts/product owners and report directly to the CPO/CTO. What you will do

  • Ensuring solutions meet business needs and requirements.
  • Working closely with the product teams (CPO, CTO and Developers) to get the solution of the product.
  • Drafting documents for business and solutions analysis.
  • Coordinating with Project Manager to manage and monitor the project progress. Supporting team members to carry out the project.
  • Taking part in User Acceptance Test along with Tester and QA.
What you need
  • At least 01 year experience as a Business Analysis.

  • Understanding the requirements quickly. Expressing information clearly. Convincing customers effectively.
  • Skill with teamwork, presentation, problem-solving.
  • Planning and time management skills.
  • Bachelor's Degree or higher, major in IT, Computer Science.
  • Solid English communication skills in both written and verbal.
